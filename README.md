# Aura

**Always-on wearable audio for memory augmentation and personal archival.**

Aura is a necklace-worn device that continuously records audio while you're awake. At night, you dock it on a charger and the day's audio uploads to a processing server. The system transcribes, identifies speakers, extracts context, and builds a living knowledge graph of your life — your connections, conversations, patterns, and memories.

The goal: augment human memory. Preserve a person's essence. Whisper forgotten names. Remind you why you walked into the room.
